About the studio

Tactile brand moments, engineered in 3D.

Redesign3D is a team of designers and print engineers making durable, customizable business cards that people remember.

Design-first process

We translate brand systems into 3D objects, calibrating emboss depth, kerning, and spacing for physical impact.

Print lab standards

Dialed-in printers, material testing, and repeatable QC so every batch feels consistent.

Human collaboration

You get direct access to the designer and technician handling your cards. Fast replies, clear proofs.

Vision

Put meaning into personal and professional identities with 3D-printed cards that stand out, feel premium, and stay memorable.

Mission

Deliver high-quality, customizable cards through an accessible, efficient platform—pairing creativity, collaboration, and reliability.

Team
