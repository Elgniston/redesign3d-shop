Resources

Guides for getting your 3D business cards right.

Save time with our quick references. Use these when preparing assets or explaining the look you want.

File prep checklist

Use vector logos (SVG preferred), outline text, and include brand colors in hex. Minimum QR size: 18mm square.

Finish picker

Matte for muted, satin for subtle sheen. Add soft chamfers for pocket-friendliness.

Color strategy

Limit to two or three tones to keep detail crisp. High contrast boosts scan reliability for QR codes.

Emboss depth tips

1.0-1.5mm for logos, 0.6-0.8mm for text. Shallower emboss keeps thin strokes clean.

Proofing expectations

We share a render, then a photographed sample under neutral light so you know exactly what you will get.

Care instructions

Store flat, avoid direct heat, and wipe with a microfiber cloth to refresh the finish.
